About Me



Zoilo is a self starting, curious, detail-oriented individual from the Bronx, New York with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Hunter College.

Fascinated with all things VR/AR/MR and video game development/design, Zoilo tinkers away at projects on Unity and other game engines in order to make quality games and experiences.

Zoilo has the following game design influences: The Legend of Zelda, Pokemon, Overwatch, Warcraft, Bioshock, Metroid Prime, as well as rogue-like indie games, such as Dungeon Souls and Dead Cells. These are just some influences, as Zoilo has a long history of playing video games.

Zoilo enjoys programming and currently teaches programming and game design at Manhattan Youth, an afterschool program. His favorite code editor is Sublime Text, and can be often found using C++,C#, or python across his projects.

When not programming, Zoilo can be found playing a video game, reading a book or comic, catching up on world news, or looking for their next project.

Zoilo is also interested in the intersections of social justice and technology, as well as machine learning and software engineering.
