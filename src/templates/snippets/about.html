About Me

Zoilo is a self starting, curious, detail-oriented individual from the Bronx, New York with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Hunter College.

Fascinated with all things VR/AR/MR and video game development/design, Zoilo tinkers away at projects on Unity and other game engines in order to make quality games and experiences.

Zoilo has the following game design influences: The Legend of Zelda, Pokemon, Overwatch, Bioshock, Metroid Prime, as well as rogue-like indie games, such as Dungeon Souls and Dead Cells. These are just some influences, as Zoilo has a long history of playing video games.

When not coding, Zoilo can be found playing a video game, reading a book or comic, catching up on world news, or looking for the next project.

Zoilo is also interested in the intersections of social justice and technology, as well as machine learning and software engineering.
